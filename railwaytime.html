                                   RAILWAY TIME TABLE OF KARAD





Karad has line station and is onroute from Mumbai to Miraj, Sangli, Kolhapur, and Bangalore (a few trains). You can arrive at Karad from Mumbai or Pune effectively by way or rail (Mahalaxmi Express, Koyna Express, Sahyadri Express or Chalukya Express). Nearest railway junction.

Miraj- 72 km

All super-quick prepares like the Karnataka Sampark Kranti, Deekshaboomi Express, Rani Chenama Express, Haripriya Express and Miraj Hubli Express stop at Miraj Junction. You can take private autos or MSRTC transports from Miraj to Karad. Travel time from Miraj to Karad is more or less 1 hour 15 minutes.
